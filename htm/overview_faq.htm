FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

  1. How much can you lift with the Manipulator?

    The manipulator can lift about 40 pounds (18.2 kg). However, if you can grab a secure hold of an object, you can retrieve it by pulling on the tether. Items weighing up to 20 kilograms (80 pounds) in water have been successfully retrieved.

  2. How much grip strength does the Manipulator have?

    The Pro 4 manipulator closes with about 4.5 kilograms (10 pounds) of force.

  3. How long does it take the manipulator to close or open fully?

    The manipulator takes 8 seconds to fully close or open.
