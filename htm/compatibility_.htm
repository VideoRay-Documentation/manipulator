Accessory Compatibility

The Manipulator is compatible with most accessories. Mounting is the only limitation.

Communications

The Manipulator uses the 9 Pin accessory port on the back of the ROV.

Function Pins Use Video 1, 2 No ROV Communications Bus 7, 8 Yes 12 V DC Power 9, 5 Yes 24 V DC Power 3, 5 Yes Spare Pair 4, 6 No Function Pins Use Video 1, 2 No Manipulator Power Bus 7, 8 Yes 12 V DC Power 9, 5 No 48 V DC Power 3, 5 No Spare Pair 4, 6 No

Mounting

The Manipulator can be mounted on the normal skid mount or on the sonar mount if the sonar is attached.
